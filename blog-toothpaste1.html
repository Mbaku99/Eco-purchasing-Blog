Toothsoap?
What is Toothsoap?

Yes, you saw that correctly, took me a few re-reads. This is a cool alternative to tubed toothpaste. Well I guess it still comes in a tube. With this trendy badboy, you can lather your toothbrush up like a traditional soap bar, to brush your teeth (hence the inventive name).

How do you use it?
  1. Run your brush under some cold water (unless you like it warm?)

  2. Get your toothsoap and rub the wet bristles over the stick till you see a lather

  3. Brush your teeth like normal
Made by who or whom?

Tis made by the fantastic Georganics, a UK (Brighton) based company which produce a variety of mouth hygene products.

What I do & How I be feeling
When and where did ya buy it?

I snagged this guy on the 18th of September 2021 through the green turtle website.
(review here)

How long does it last?

Well... i've had this guy for exactly 109 days, and I must say that I am impressed. I had hoped to be using the toothsoap while travelling to China, however it was cancelled (damn that COVID sucker) but its still coming in handy.
At first, just like many toothpastes I wa using a heavy amount, (in comparsion to what I use now) to make sure I was getting enough on my teeth while brushing. But I soon realised that only a small amount was enough to lather up and do good! I now just move my toothbrush around the stick about twice till I see it lathered on the brush.

Tips and Tricks?

I was today years old when I realised you could push the bottom of the tube to raise it up so that when the upper parts get exhausted, you can still use the tube without taking the toothsoap out. This wasn't described on the website so I am fully amazed right now!
