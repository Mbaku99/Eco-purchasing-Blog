About me

🌎Introduction

Hi there, my name is Wilf (see picture) and I am an avid "environmentalist" even if that is a word. Even though I believe in collective and industry level change in sustainability, I think it is important to apply this to our lifestyles, if not for our planet then for our health. Using organic, natural and waste-less materials are key to maintain a sustainable lifestyle and thats what I hope to be presenting here! Enjoy

🌎Education

I went to the University of Reading where I studied 'Environmental Management', so while I am not an expert I know some of my stuff getting to know a range of topics such as climate change, biology, conservation, land management, urban greenspace, GIS, pollution (water, air, soil)
